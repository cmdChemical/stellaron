Welcome aboard Calympia

Calympia is Stellaron's story and lore. This story is about a character, Echo, who went through a lot of hardship. Neglected by his father, he goes through a harsh journey to defy his father and make the world a better place for his friends.

Calympia's Network

Calympia is a big city where a few millions of people live. As part of Calympia's modernisation plan, created by Captain Echo, an underground system was created where "endless" bus routes are no more. You can now travel through Calympia seamlessly with Calympia's own underground network, and even faster with its Express network.

Stellar Corps: Captains

The Stellar Corps is an organization that has protected Calympia from Delusions for centuries. The current amount of Swordsmen and Swordswomen are in thousands. The Stellar Corps have Seven Divisions, and Two Elite Divisions. Each Division is led by a Captain or by an Officer.

Captain Echo
The Sound Of The Raging Justice

  • Primary Element: Lunar
  • Secondary Element: Solar

    • After living an arduous and joyless childhood, Echo assumes his role as Captain. Broken heart and broken hope, he restrains his genius and capabilities a lot. Despite that, his real strength and aptitude are immeasurable. No one knows his true ambitions, nor his limits. A lot of people misjudge and hate Echo for his "neutrality", "emotionless behaviour", and "weird physique" - though his friends and Stellar Corps colleagues say otherwise.

    Captain Aphan
    The Radiant Dawn of Earth

  • Primary Element: Earth
  • Secondary Element: Solar

    • Aphan is a person who has an incredible talent. Her father used to serve Calympia's Stellar Corps as a Stellaron Head Officer, and her mother used to be an Administrator at Captains' Park who was in charge of Scientific and Technological Progress. After the unfortunate event of her father in a mission, her mother took care of her until she turned 18. Shattered, Aphan decides to take back control of her life. She wishes one day to fulfil all of his younger brother's wishes and make sure he enjoys his life to the fullest.

    Captain Firey
    The Everlasting Flame Of Bravery

  • Primary Element: Fire
  • Secondary Element: Solar

    • Firey, a man who enjoys every single bit of his life. Living a somewhat unique past, being different and being himself led to some more "difficult" moments during his life. However, he managed to get through it all, and now as Captain, he will pursue his dreams, set his heart ablaze, protect his loved ones, and do the best he can as his true self.

    Captain Rose
    The Delightful Blossom

  • Element: Nature

    • A woman and sister whose heart is the kindest and sweetest of all. Rose goes through many hardships at a very early age, fulfilling her younger brother's dreams even if it means working twice, and even thrice as hard. She has her own small circle of friends she spends time with and she is fond of, and will do everything to protect them from pain at all costs. Despite not being the strongest or having the natural appearance of a Captain, she is extremely smart and strong.

    Captain Ash
    The Ocean Of Fairness and Hope

  • Primary Element: Water

    • The man who pursues his dreams like no other. Ash may be a very enthusiastic person, but he always makes sure to succeed or make nearly perfect every thing he does. While trying to achieve the best out of himself, he always makes sure to be as fair and as precise as possible. He may not be the strongest, but his intelligence surpasses his strength.

    Captain Omar
    The Ambassador Of The Permafrost

  • Primary Element: Ice

    • One whose pride is his heart and his courage is his strength. Omar not only is proud of his life; he's also proud of his loved ones. One of the most courageous and righteous people among the Stellar Corps, he'll go over his limits to protect his loved ones, his pride, and make regret those who hurt or dare to hurt him and his loved ones.

    Captain Beatrice
    The Electrifying Ambition

  • Primary Element: Electro

    • She may not be the strongest, but her ambitions and her heart forges her identity, thus leading her to achievements that most people are never able to achieve. Beatrice is the last Captain up to date. She managed to get through her own hardships, faced many challenges, and will continue to follow her path to her own success.
